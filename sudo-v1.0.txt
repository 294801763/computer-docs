给用户增加sudo
-------------------
$>vi /etc/sudoers
--
...
hy    ALL=(ALL:ALL) ALL
...

$>su - hy 
$>sudo whoami

sudo免密
-------------------
$>sudo visudo
--
username ALL=(ALL) NOPASSWD: ALL                                         #全部免密.
or
username ALL=(ALL) NOPASSWD: /usr/bin/apt-get                            #APT-GET免密.

eg.
--
hy    ALL=(ALL) NOPASSWD: ALL
